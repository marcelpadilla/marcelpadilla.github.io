Mathematicians Reimagined

A private side project, winter 2023
Marcel Padilla
TU Berlin

Abstract

I was fooling around with Ai image generators and came across stable diffusion models and extensions which allowed me to generate new images of famous mathematicians using existing creative commons images as input. I present here a few of my results.

I will upload some more images soon and also future tools will allow me to upgrade some of these images.

Carl Friedrich Gauss reimagined in a modern look. The image on the left is public domain.

Leonhard Euler reimagined. The image on the left is public domain. The center image focuses on maintinging the painting stlye.
The right image is Leon-Hard Euler. Yes, the hat-wear is weird.

Workflow

  • Stable diffusion 1.5 img2img
  • ControlNet Canny + ip-adapter-full-face with
  • ReActor face swapping
  • Minor fixes and upscaling

Note

I personally make no copy right claims on this output, and advice you to be careful regarding copyrights of these images as they are output of ai models that were trained on unknown data sets. You can however still cite this webpage using the bibtext below.
