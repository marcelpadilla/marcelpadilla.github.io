Mathematicians Reimagined

A private side project by Marcel Padilla - Winter 2023

Abstract

I was fooling around with AI image generators and came across stable diffusion models and extensions that allowed me to generate modern images of famous mathematicians using old existing public domain images as input. I present here a few of my results. The people displayed here are sorted by birth year.

Workflow

  • Stable diffusion 1.5 img2img
  • ControlNet with various settings
  • ReActor face swapping
  • Lots and lots of fixes

I can't really be more specific without going into endless explanations when to use which ControlNet model with what settings. No two examples here were made with the same pipeline. I had to test my way around to get images I am satisfied with. Many ideas were not realized, especially regarding hair and beard features.
