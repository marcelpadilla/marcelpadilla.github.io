Nailing the Bunny

A private side project, 2022
Marcel Padilla
TU Berlin

Abstract

Take white board, hammer some nails into it, and connect the nails to form the visualisation of a 3D mesh.

I present here the short story of how this project turned out.

Carl Friedrich Gauss reimagined with a modern look. The image on the left is public domain.

Workflow

  • Stable diffusion 1.5 img2img starting with a public domain image.
  • Creating a text prompt that describes what I want to see in the end.
  • ControlNet canny + ip-adapter using a public domain image.
  • ReActor face swapping using a public domain image.
  • Minor fixes and upscaling.
